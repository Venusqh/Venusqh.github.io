Some Interesting Life Moments

Specimens of bee genera from the Halictidae family collected and identified at McMaster University West Campus in summer 2022 for my undergrad thesis project

Genus General characteristics
Agapostemon Largest of the green sweat bees. Abdomen usually with a striped pattern, male is yellow and black, and female is white-black or solid green.
Augochlora Tegula is clearly oval; Subantennal sutures are narrow bands with acute angle
Augochlorella Smallest of the green; Subantennal sutures are broad black bands with obtuse or perpendicular angle
Halictus Metasoma with hair bands on apical portion of each tergum
Lasioglossum No facial fovea; metasoma with hair bands absent or at the bottom of tergum
Sphecodes Abdomen in red, metasoma with hair bands absent or at the bottom of tergum
